Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

Types of Data Collected

Personal Data You Provide

When you register or use the Service, we may ask you to provide:

  • Email address
  • First name (and optionally last name)
  • Date of Birth, Time of Birth, Place of Birth, Gender, and Name — to generate and maintain personalized horoscope and astrological services. Your horoscope and birth chart are stored securely with us.
  • Optional life events you choose to share (for example marriage, job change, childbirth) to improve personalization and predictive accuracy.
  • Questions you ask and AI-generated answers, including remedies or guidance, to maintain continuity and improve your experience.

Usage Data

Usage Data is collected automatically when you use the Service. It may include:

  • Device information (model, OS version, unique device identifiers)
  • IP address and general network information
  • App interactions (screens viewed, features used, crash logs, diagnostic data)
  • Approximate location inferred from IP address (only for security, fraud prevention, and regional experience defaults; we do not collect precise GPS location unless you explicitly enable a feature that needs it)

Tracking Technologies and Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies on our website and, where applicable, in the App to keep you signed in, remember preferences, and understand Service performance. You can control cookies via your browser settings. The App does not use cookies to track you across other apps or services.

Android App Permissions

The App may request certain permissions to provide features you choose to use. We only access these permissions when needed for that feature:

  • Internet / Network Access (required): to connect to our servers and deliver predictions and chats.
  • Storage (required for maintaining sessions): to store basic app data locally (such as login/session information) so you stay signed in and the App works reliably. We do not access your personal files.
  • Notifications (optional): to send reminders, updates, or consultation alerts. You can disable these in device settings.
  • Location (GPS) (optional, only for a specific feature): if you enable a feature that needs precise location, we will access GPS only while that feature is in use. We do not collect precise location in the background.

Note: This list must match the permissions in your AndroidManifest. If your build does not use a permission, remove it from both the manifest and this policy.

How We Use Your Data

We use your data only for internal purposes to operate and improve the Service, including:

  • Provide and maintain the Service: personalized horoscope, astrological insights, AI chat, account access, and customer support.
  • Personalization: to tailor predictions and guidance based on your birth details and optional life events.
  • Improve quality and safety: debugging, performance analytics, fraud prevention, and security monitoring.
  • Communications: sending service‑related messages such as OTPs, important updates, and support replies. Marketing messages (if any) will include an opt‑out.
  • AI model improvement (internal use only): we may use anonymized or pseudonymized birth details, life events, and chat history to improve our AI models. This data is never used for third‑party advertising or profiling. You may opt out of this internal AI‑training use by emailing us at info@aipandit.co.in.

We do not sell your data. We do not share your Personal Data with third‑party businesses for their marketing, advertising, or other independent use.

We may share data only in these limited cases:

  • With Service Providers: to help us operate the Service (for example cloud hosting, database storage, email/SMS delivery, crash/analytics, and payment processing). Service Providers may process data only under our instructions and are not permitted to use it for their own purposes.
  • Business transfers: if we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you if your data becomes subject to a different policy.
  • Legal requirements: if required by law or valid governmental request, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of users and the Company.
  • With your consent: for any other purpose you explicitly agree to.

Retention of Your Personal Data

We retain your Personal Data only as long as needed for the purposes described above:

  • Chat history and horoscope charts may be retained to provide continuity unless you delete them or request deletion.
  • Usage Data is retained for internal analytics and security, typically for shorter durations unless required longer by law.
